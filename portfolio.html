PATRICK DAVID REINHART

Web Developer Graphic Designer

Background

I am an ever-developing web developer and graphic designer. I taught myself HTML starting from the moment we turned on our first family computer and I found out AOL provided a free megabyte of space to make a webpage. Thus, I grew up designing fan pages in raw HTML, uploaded through FTP on places like FortuneCity and Tripod, and other long since defunct platforms.

Similarly I have always been fascinated by the usage and evolution of blogging platforms and social media (shoutouts to LiveJournal, Yahoo! Groups, Blogspot, Wordpress, Tumblr, Diaspora, Ello, Pillowfort, as well as the usual suspects).

Projects

I am currently developing Curiouser House Publishing in my spare time, redesigning and republishing classic literature in uniform editions.

Throughout my time I have helped develop and design websites for: a Thai restaurant, an Americana band, a Waldorf school, TenorGuitar.com, a startup company that designed interactive children’s book apps, a costume and vintage clothing shop, and Tom The Book Guy, where I worked for nearly seven years.
